#  About

I navigate how democratic states in Northeast Asia build and deploy strategic state capacity in industrial and technological transformation. 

My focus is not just on de-risking transitions, but how it impacts industrial strategic interests in: 
1) Energy Security: Analyzing decarbonization pathways and grid resilience.

2) Geopolitical Risk: Navigating the US-China strategic competition and regulatory shifts. 

3) EV & Battery Supply Chains: Decoding state-led industrial strategies and critical mineral access.

Current: Senior Research Specialist at the Asia Pacific Foundation of Canada (APFC).

Background: Ph.D. in political science (UBC).

Outside the data, I am a dedicated swimmer and amateur pianist.
